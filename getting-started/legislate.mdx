Legislate is TextMine's document intelligence and management module. It is where verified document data from Vault lands, where new documents are created from Scribe templates, and where you can search, filter, analyse, and export data across your entire document library.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore your document database">
    When you open a Legislate team, you see a table of all the documents in that team. Each row represents one document, and the columns show the key data points (tags) extracted by Vault or filled in via Scribe.

    Use the **filter** controls to narrow the list by document type, tag value, date range, or any other field. You can combine multiple filters to answer questions like "show me all NDAs expiring before the end of the year."

    <Tip>Use the column selector to show or hide specific tags in the table view, keeping the view focused on the data you care about most.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a document">
    Click any document row to open the detail view. Here you can:

    - Read the original document.
    - See all extracted or entered tag values.
    - View the document's status (for example, draft, signed, or submitted).
    - Download the document.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a document from a template">
    Click **New document** and browse the templates published by your Scribe authors. Select a template and complete the form — each field corresponds to a variable in the template.

    Once you submit the form, Legislate generates the document and adds it to your team library.

    <Info>Only templates that have been published to your specific Legislate team will appear in the template list. Ask your Scribe template author to share the template with your team if you cannot find it.</Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send for signature">
    Open a generated or uploaded document and click **Send for signature**. Add the signatories by name and email address, set the signing order if required, and send. Signatories receive an email invitation to review and sign.

    You can track the signing status — pending, signed, or declined — directly in the document detail view.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export your data">
    To export document data for further analysis, apply the filters you need and click **Export**. Legislate downloads the visible data as a CSV file, ready for use in spreadsheets or other tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>
