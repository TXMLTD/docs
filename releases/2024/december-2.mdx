title: "TextMine — December 2, 2024 release"
description: "Pagination extended to all document types including scans, and Legislate documents can now be resubmitted directly to Vault for fresh data extraction."
---

The December 2, 2024 release extends pagination coverage to every document type and adds the ability to move Legislate documents back to Vault.

## Universal pagination

In the original pagination release, pagination was not available for certain documents such as scans. Pagination is now available for any document uploaded to Vault.

## Submit documents from Legislate to Vault

Documents in Legislate can now be resubmitted to Vault. This is useful if you would like to extract new data from existing documents. You can send a document back to Vault by clicking the three-dot menu in Legislate and selecting **"Move document to Vault"**.
