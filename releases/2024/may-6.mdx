The May 6, 2024 release ships Scribe bug fixes, styling improvements, and expanded document type support in Vault.

TextMine has released the following updates:

- Scribe bug fixes related to the creation of fields and the importing of Word templates
- Scribe styling improvements and functionality
- Support in Vault for 10-K and 20-F documents
