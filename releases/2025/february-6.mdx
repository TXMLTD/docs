The February 6, 2025 release improves bulk importing and adds new download options in Legislate.

## Uploading

TextMine has implemented improved bulk importing functionality which allows you to carry on with your tasks in TextMine whilst uploads are happening.

A preview of the uploads appears in the bottom right and allows you to continue with any ongoing tasks within the platform.

## Downloading

Data in Legislate can now be downloaded directly via the selector.

You also now have the option to download the annotated PDFs for selected files.
