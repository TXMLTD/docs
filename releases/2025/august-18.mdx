The 18 August 2025 release introduces organisation-level analytics, giving admins visibility into document processing status, accuracy, and usage across all business units.

## Analytics

Organisations can now track high-level analytics across their business units. These analytics include:

- The status of documents uploaded to Vault
- The overall accuracy by document type
- The number of pages processed by their team

![Organisation analytics dashboard](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1676099151/27fbcb38acb1f6aad841ce2a322a/Screenshot+2025-08-18+at+17_38_53.png)

The tracking period for each chart can be changed, and your preference will be saved automatically based on your selection.

![Tracking period selector on a chart](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1676100277/21c2b6edba1956d06de1578ba867/Screenshot+2025-08-18+at+17_39_35.png)

To enable analytics for a specific role, the organisation admin will need to enable the **View Vault analytics** permission under Vault permissions.
