---
title: "TextMine — 17 December 2025 release"
description: "Adds reasoning tags, improved agent results with source links and CSV export, a configurable Legislate team view, and the ability to delete personal teams."
---

The 17 December 2025 release adds reasoning tags, enhanced agent results with export capability, an improved Legislate team view, and the ability to delete personal working teams.

## Reasoning tags

TextMine now offers reasoning tags for both custom tags and default document type tags. They can also be applied to aggregation tags.

![Reasoning tag option in the tag configuration panel](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889952102/6399e5371216dc0cd36dabc5a6dd/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+07_47_05.png)

When enabled, the model will provide additional reasoning explaining how it found the answer. You can choose to include the reasoning in the answer.

![Reasoning displayed alongside a tag answer](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889952419/66568bfaa23a3c889f6c48d47a17/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+08_02_41.png)

![Reasoning detail in the tag card](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889952674/a6d1378b3a8ef6aae865519b6848/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+08_02_51.png)

## Agent results and exports

Agent results now present links to the source files and expandable cells to better interpret the results. The results table can also be exported to CSV.

![Agent results table with source links and expandable cells](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889964592/bfa4f15878b1b1f19b6f8745c355/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+08_06_22.png)

![CSV export option on the agent results table](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889966023/29b7d3b1e8c3e9f0849bd27a2daf/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+08_06_34.png)

## Improved Legislate team view

The Legislate team view now offers configurable columns and allows you to view when documents were published from Vault to Legislate, as well as the assignee and export date.

![Configurable columns in the Legislate team view](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889913280/e19f5e34d1b5213ec67b91c588b2/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+07_40_24.png)

![Legislate team view showing publish date, assignee, and export date](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1889913033/a398a473453c9432fddbf1fa7604/Screenshot+2025-12-17+at+07_40_35.png)

## Delete personal working teams

You can now delete working (personal) teams as long as they don't have any documents in either Vault or Legislate.

<Note>
  Business units and teams connected to an organisation cannot be deleted.
</Note>
