The February 20, 2025 release introduces archiving and deletion for documents in Legislate.

## Archiving

It is now possible to archive and delete documents in Legislate.

Each team in Legislate now displays an **Active** and **Archived** tab to help you better organise your documents.

Documents can be moved to the Archive section by selecting them and clicking on the Archive icon.

You can access archived documents by clicking on the Archived tab, where you can either permanently delete a document or move it back to the Active section.
