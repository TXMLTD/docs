title: "TextMine — May 21, 2025 release"
description: "New permission to create personal teams, updated copy for tag insertion and Vault settings labels, and the Settings section renamed to Custom Types."
---

The May 21, 2025 release brings a permission addition, several copy clarifications, and a navigation rename inside Vault.

## New permission: allow users to create a personal team

A new permission has been added that allows users to create a personal team.

## Copy updates

Two labels have been updated to better describe their actions:

- **"Create default tag"** is now: **"Manually insert a tag in the plain text"**
- **"Edit default settings"** is now: **"Edit the default settings of a Vault Business Unit"**

## Settings section renamed to "Custom Types"

The Settings section under Vault in the sidebar has been renamed to **Custom Types** to avoid confusion with the Vault Settings of a Business Unit.
