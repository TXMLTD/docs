---

The February 14, 2025 release delivers a substantially improved Vault dashboard, tag highlighting in the PDF preview, a full reviewer workflow, and new validation controls.

## Vault Dashboard

The Vault dashboard now provides greater visibility into document uploads. This now includes the upload date, uploader name, reviewer name, and review date. All columns can be used to sort the documents in Vault.

## Tag highlighting

Within a document, it is now possible to see highlighted tags in the PDF preview. This was previously only possible in the plain text.

<Note>
Tag highlighting may not work on scanned documents. The search will always work on the Plain Text tab.
</Note>

You can also use the search bar to navigate through the document. Using your keyboard's arrows you can switch between answers.

## Reviewers

### Assigning reviewers

You can now assign uploaded documents to reviewers.

#### Assign reviewers in bulk

Select the relevant documents and assign the reviewer.

#### Assign reviewers one by one

Click on the reviewer label and assign a reviewer. You can also un-assign or re-assign a document's reviewer at any time.

### Audit trail

The review date now appears next to individual tags when a field is validated. The document's overall review date also appears in the dashboard.

### Validation settings

Within the Vault settings it is now possible to enforce the review strategy by enabling the **"Only reviewers can validate fields"** setting. This means that unassigned tags cannot be reviewed by users who are not assigned to the document.
