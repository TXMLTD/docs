title: "TextMine — 26 November 2025 release"
description: "Adds auto-assignment of documents to yourself at upload time, and PDF export improvements including validator name display and more accurate tag page matching."
---

The 26 November 2025 release introduces auto-assignment at upload and improves PDF exports with validator names and better tag page placement.

## Auto-assign

You can now auto-assign documents to yourself at the time of upload.

![Auto-assign toggle in the upload settings](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1850592642/911d634e917a86b80c7e9e788a1b/Screenshot+2025-11-26+at+07_25_15.png)

This works for both direct uploads and integrations.

## Export enhancements

The PDF export now displays the name of the user who validated the tags and has more accurate tag page matching.

![PDF export showing validator name and accurate page references](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h2osv3w3/1850595152/9b9c1adb1ded5656b9f1afa5dc67/Screenshot+2025-11-26+at+07_27_06.png)
