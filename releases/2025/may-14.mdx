The May 14, 2025 release introduces breadcrumb navigation across the application.

## Breadcrumbs

We have improved the navigation experience by introducing breadcrumbs at the top of the page.

Breadcrumbs help users quickly assess where they are in the application and easily navigate to their desired teams or subfolders by clicking on the breadcrumb heading.
