Legislate is TextMine's document creation and management workspace. It's where your broader organisation — HR, sales, operations, and other teams — creates, signs, and tracks documents generated from Scribe templates. You don't need access to Scribe to use Legislate; everything you need is surfaced through simple forms and dashboards.

## What you can do in Legislate

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create documents" icon="file-plus">
    Generate contracts and documents from approved Scribe templates by filling in a guided form. No drafting experience required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage parties and signatories" icon="user-check">
    Add companies or individuals as parties to a document, assign signatories, and include collaborators or witnesses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign documents" icon="pen-nib">
    Initiate the signing process once all terms and parties are in place. Draw or upload a signature and track the status of all signatures in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Filter your entire document library by the data fields within documents. Export matching results as a CSV for reporting and audits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom alerts" icon="bell">
    Set up email notifications for key document events — expiry, notice periods, renewal dates, and review deadlines — delivered to the right people at the right time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Document data exploration" icon="chart-bar">
    Legislate surfaces the structured data extracted from your documents, giving you real visibility into what your agreements actually say across the portfolio.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Legislate connects to the rest of TextMine

Legislate sits at the centre of TextMine's document lifecycle, connecting with both Scribe and Vault.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Legislate and Scribe">
    Scribe is where legal or compliance teams build and maintain document templates. When a Scribe template is published to a Legislate team, users in that team can create document instances from it via a simple form. Legislate users never need to edit the template text themselves — the form handles everything.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Legislate and Vault">
    Vault is TextMine's document intelligence layer, which reads and extracts structured data from uploaded documents. The data Vault verifies flows into Legislate's search and reporting capabilities, so when you filter documents by a specific clause value or date, you're working with verified, machine-readable data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Access to Legislate is separate from access to Scribe. Your Scribe administrator controls which templates are available to which Legislate teams. If you don't see a template you expect, ask your Scribe editor to publish it to your team.
</Note>
