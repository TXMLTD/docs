Ask Vault is a conversational interface built into Vault that lets you interrogate, transform, and analyse the data your documents contain. Instead of filtering columns manually, you type a question and Ask Vault answers it using the extracted tag data from your Vault team.

## Opening Ask Vault

You can open Ask Vault in two ways:

- Press **Q** on your keyboard from anywhere within a Vault team.
- Click the **Ask Vault** button in the Vault interface.

The chat panel opens on the right side of the screen. You can also pop it out into a separate window for more space by clicking the pop-out icon.

## What you can do

Ask Vault supports natural language questions and actions across several categories:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Query documents">
    Ask questions about the data in your documents. For example:

    - "Which supplier agreements expire in the next 90 days?"
    - "What is the governing law across all NDAs in this vault?"
    - "Show me all invoices over £10,000."

    Each answer includes **source documents** linked directly in the Vault, so you can cross-reference the answer with the original file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Generate tables">
    Ask for a structured view of your data and Ask Vault will produce a table. Tables can be exported to **CSV** or **XLSX** directly from the chat.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Generate plots">
    Ask Vault to visualise data from a table. It will produce a chart you can export. For example: "Plot the contract values by document type."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Query failed documents">
    Ask which documents failed to process or had low confidence scores, so you can prioritise which ones need manual review.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Assign and unassign documents">
    Ask Vault to assign a document to a team member or unassign it. Actions that make changes will always ask you to **confirm before executing**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reprocess or change a document type">
    Ask Vault to reprocess a document or change its detected type. As with all actions, you will be asked to confirm.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Run reports and agents">
    Trigger reports or run agents on the documents in your vault through the chat interface.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How responses work

Every answer is presented with references to the source documents used to produce it. Click a source reference to open the corresponding document in Vault.

For any action that modifies data (assignment, reprocessing, type changes), Ask Vault will present a confirmation step before making the change.

## Limitations to be aware of

<Warning>Ask Vault chats are not saved. If you refresh the page or switch to a different team or screen, the chat history is lost. Copy or export any outputs you want to keep before navigating away.</Warning>

<Note>Ask Vault is scoped to the team you are currently viewing. It only has access to documents within that Vault team — not documents in other teams or in Legislate.</Note>
