Validating predictions is the core review step in Vault. Before a document's data can be submitted to Legislate, every extracted tag must be validated — either by confirming the AI's answer is correct or by correcting it first. This ensures that only verified data enters your document database.

## Opening the review screen

Click on any document in your Vault team list to open the review screen. The uploaded PDF appears on the left; the list of extracted tags appears on the right.

<Tip>Click any tag in the right-hand panel to jump to the section of the PDF the AI used to produce that value. This makes spot-checking fast without requiring you to read the whole document.</Tip>

## Validating tags one by one

For each tag in the panel on the right:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the value">
    Read the extracted value and, if needed, click the tag to see the source text highlighted in the PDF.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Correct if needed">
    If the value is incorrect or needs reformatting, click the **pen icon** to edit it. Type the correct value and press Enter or click away to save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate">
    Once the value is correct, click the **tick** to validate the tag. The tag moves to a validated state.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can return to a validated tag and make further edits by clicking the pen icon again.

## Validating all tags at once

If you have reviewed the document and are confident all predictions are correct, click the **All** (validate all) button in the top right of the editor. This validates every unvalidated tag in one action.

<Warning>Use bulk validation only when you have reviewed the document and are confident in the extracted values. Validated data is treated as verified once submitted to Legislate.</Warning>

## Submitting to Legislate

The **Submit** button becomes active only after every tag has been validated (or rejected). Click **Submit** to push the document and its verified data to your paired Legislate team.

<Info>If the Submit button remains greyed out, at least one tag still needs a decision. Scroll through the tag list to find any that are still in the unvalidated state.</Info>
