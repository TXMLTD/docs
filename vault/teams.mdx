Vault organises your document imports into teams. Every Vault team is paired with a Legislate team, creating a clear two-stage pipeline: documents arrive in Vault for review, and verified data flows into Legislate for analysis and management.

## Why teams are separated

The separation between Vault and Legislate is intentional. It distinguishes two states of your data:

- **Vault (review data)** — documents that have been imported but whose extracted tags have not yet been fully validated. This data is still being checked and may contain errors.
- **Legislate (verified data)** — documents that have been reviewed, corrected, and submitted. This data has been confirmed as accurate and is ready for analysis, filtering, and export.

Keeping these two states separate ensures you can act confidently on data in Legislate without worrying that unreviewed extractions are mixed in.

## Creating a Vault team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the + icon">
    In the left-hand sidebar, click the **+** button next to the Vault icon. A pop-up appears with two options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how to connect the team">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Connect to an existing Legislate team">
        Select this option if your organisation already has a Legislate team you want Vault imports to flow into. Choose the Legislate team from the list.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Create a new team">
        Select this option to create both a new Vault team and a new paired Legislate team simultaneously. Enter a name for the team.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start uploading">
    Once the team is created, you can begin uploading documents to it immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens when you submit a document

When you validate all tags on a document in Vault and click **Submit**, the document and its verified data are transferred to the paired Legislate team. In Legislate, the document can then be:

- Filtered and searched alongside your other documents.
- Included in aggregate queries and analysis.
- Exported as a CSV for use in other tools.

<Info>Data in Vault is scoped to the team it was imported into. When you submit a document, it moves into the paired Legislate team and is no longer treated as pending review.</Info>
