TextMine is an end-to-end knowledge base for your business-critical documents. It combines three powerful modules — **Vault**, **Scribe**, and **Legislate** — to help you extract data from existing documents, create new documents from smart templates, and explore all your document data in one place.

## What is TextMine?

TextMine has three core modules that work together:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Vault" icon="vault" href="/vault/overview">
    AI-powered document data extraction. Upload PDFs and Vault automatically classifies each document and extracts key data fields with confidence scores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scribe" icon="pen-nib" href="/scribe/overview">
    Smart document templates. Build reusable templates with dynamic fields, conditional logic, and collaborative editing — then publish them to your teams.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Legislate" icon="scale-balanced" href="/legislate/overview">
    Document data exploration and lifecycle management. Search, filter, and export verified document data, create documents from Scribe templates, and manage document signing.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the modules connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Extract data with Vault">
    Upload your existing business documents to Vault. The AI classifies each document, extracts key data points, and presents them for your review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate and submit">
    Review the AI's predictions, make any corrections, and submit verified documents to Legislate — your trusted source of document data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore and analyse">
    Use Legislate's advanced search to filter documents by their extracted data, answer aggregate queries, and export results as CSV for further analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new documents">
    Use Scribe to build smart templates. Publish them to Legislate teams so anyone can generate a correctly-formatted document just by filling out a form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
